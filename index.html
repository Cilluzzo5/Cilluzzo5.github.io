Intro

Hi! I'm Nicola Cilli, a Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer passionate about business solutions, problem-solving, and the intellectual challenges that our field presents.

I graduated in Computer Science from the University of Milan-Bicocca. I completed my Bachelor's degree in 2020, alongside an exciting project in the automotive industry, which resulted in a publication in the prestigious Springer journal. Following that, I started my Master's degree while working as a Data Science Consultant for a renowned Milan-based company. I completed my Master's degree on time in 2022 with a thesis on an ensemble model for fraud detection.

My career has always been driven by a strong sense of initiative, allowing me to take on increasing levels of responsibility. Specifically, I've always aimed to become a Data Scientist with a focus on putting models into production! Real-time models, batch processing, synchronous and asynchronous communication, database synchronization, event-driven systems—everything that surrounds a model excites me as much as building the model itself.

Today, I am a proud technical and project leader, managing a portfolio of clients while eager to explore the more strategic aspects of my work. This side involves communication and processes that culminate in an AI project. To nurture this curiosity, I also study finance in my free time. I am a huge fan of my career because it allows me to do something I love: help others. And helping others, scientifically in this case, fills my days with purpose and brings new, exciting challenges.
